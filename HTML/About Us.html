Together, We Spark Changes

ProjectSpark is a youth-led organization founded in 2025 in Pekanbaru, Indonesia. We are dedicated to educating, empowering, and uplifting underprivileged communities through meaningful actions.

Turning Sparks into Actions

At ProjectSpark, we believe real change begins with small actions. Our initiatives focus on education, sustainability, and community empowerment to create a lasting impact for underprivileged childrens in Pekanbaru.
